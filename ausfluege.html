FAQ

Day trips from Trogir — common questions

What are the best day trips from Trogir?

The most popular are Krka waterfalls, the Hvar and Blue Cave speedboat tour, Plitvice Lakes, the Blue Lagoon and a half-day in Split. Krka and the Blue Lagoon are the easiest; Plitvice is the longest day.

Should you take a tour or drive the day trips yourself?

Organised tours include transfer and tickets with no logistics. Self-driving gives more flexibility and can be cheaper for a family — a hire car at Split Airport is easy to collect on arrival.

What is the best boat trip from Trogir?

For a relaxed day, the Blue Lagoon and islands trip; for the showpiece, the full-day Blue Cave and Hvar speedboat tour. See our Trogir boat tours guide to compare group trips and private charters.

How far is Split Airport from Trogir?

Just a few kilometres — about a 5 to 10 minute drive, closer than Split city itself. See our Split Airport to Trogir guide for transfer, taxi, bus and car options.
