Location

Where to stay in Trogir

Location makes or breaks your trip. These are the four areas worth knowing before you book — or jump to our best hotels in Trogir picks. Still choosing a town? See Trogir or Split.

Most romantic

Old Town (island)

In the heart of the UNESCO site, everything on foot. Narrow stone lanes, lively evenings. Few parking spaces, and some old buildings have no air conditioning.

Walk to centre: 0 min£££
Quietest

Čiovo

Just across the bridge from the old town, yet noticeably calmer. More hotels with pools, often a private beach, and easier parking.

Seget Donji / Vranjica

Insider tips

Before you book

01

Book early for July & August

In peak season the good places are often gone by April. If you're coming in summer, book by spring at the latest.

02

Choose free cancellation

Weather and plans change. When filtering, look for "free cancellation" — it usually costs no more.

03

Old town means no car at the door

No cars are allowed inside the old town. If you're driving, pick a hotel with parking or stay on Čiovo or the mainland.

FAQ

Trogir hotels — common questions

Where is the best area to stay in Trogir?

The old town (the island) is best for atmosphere with everything on foot; Čiovo is quieter with more pools and easier parking; Seget and Vranjica on the mainland are the best value. See our best hotels in Trogir guide for picks by area.

How do you get from Split Airport to a Trogir hotel?

Split Airport is only 5–10 minutes from Trogir. Pre-book a private transfer or take a taxi; for full options see our Split Airport to Trogir guide, or compare a hire car at Split Airport if you plan to explore.

Do hotels in Trogir old town have parking?

No cars enter the car-free old town, so pick a hotel with a parking arrangement or stay on Čiovo or the mainland and walk in. If you are driving, a hire car is easy to collect at Split Airport on arrival.

When should you book a hotel in Trogir?

For July and August, book by spring — the best places sell out early. Choose a rate with free cancellation so you can lock in a good price now and stay flexible.
